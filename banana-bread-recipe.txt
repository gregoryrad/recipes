Ingredients:
 - 1 cup GF flour
 - 3/4 cup almond flour
 - 2 Tbsp coconut flour
 - 1/2 cup coconut sugar
 - 2 tsp baking powder
 - 1/2 tsp baking soda
 - 1/4 tsp salt
 - 1/4 tsp xantham gum
 - 3 mashed ripe bananas
 - 1/3 cup applesauce
 - 2 Tbsp milk
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/4-1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

 2. Grease a bread loaf pan.

 3. Add batter to pan and bake for 25 - 35 minutes
 or until a knife stuck in the center comes out clean
